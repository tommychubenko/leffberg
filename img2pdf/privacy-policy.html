Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Too Simple: Photo to PDF application (hereinafter referred to as the "App") handles your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We respect your privacy and **do not collect**, store, or transfer any personal information (such as name, phone number, or contacts) to our own servers.

2. Device Permissions (Camera and Photos)

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used exclusively to allow you to take photos of documents directly within the app for conversion to PDF.
  • Photo Library (Gallery): Used to select existing images for conversion.

Important: All images are processed locally on your device. We do not have access to your photos outside of the conversion process.

3. Local Storage (Async Storage)

The App uses your device's local storage to save your settings and temporary data. This data remains on your phone and is deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Advertising and Analytics (Third-Party Services)

To support the free version of the App, we use third-party services that may collect technical device information:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID) to show relevant ads. You can review their policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.
  • RevenueCat: In future updates, the App will use this service to manage subscriptions and purchases. All payment information is processed through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect information from children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly interact with the personal data of children.

6. Data Deletion Instructions

Since Too Simple: Photo to PDF does not require account creation and does not store personal data (like name or email) on its servers, there is no personal account data to delete. However:

  • Local Data: To delete all data stored locally by the App, simply uninstall the App from your device.
  • Advertising ID: You can reset or delete your advertising identifier through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • General Requests: If you believe any other data has been collected, you can send a deletion request to leffberg@gmail.com. We will process your request within 48 hours.
