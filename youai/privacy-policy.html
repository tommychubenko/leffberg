Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how YOU.AI (hereinafter referred to as the "App", "Service", or "we") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our AI-powered photo and video generation application.

Quick Summary: We respect your privacy. We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or phone number. We only collect minimal technical data necessary for the App to function, such as an anonymous device identifier and usage statistics. Your generated content is not stored on our servers after delivery to your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account credentials or authentication data
  • Your generated photos and videos (after delivery to your device)
  • Your input photos used for AI processing (used only temporarily during generation)
  • Location data (precise location)
  • Contact lists or other personal data from your device
  • Biometric data

1.2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the following minimal technical information:

  • Device Identifier: An anonymous, stable device identifier (Android ID on Android devices, Identifier for Vendor (IDFV) on iOS devices) used to restore your app state, energy balance, and purchases across app reinstalls. This identifier cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Push Notification Token: A token required to send you push notifications about completed generations and app updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Usage Data: Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics such as:
    • Number of generations performed
    • Energy balance and usage
    • App version and platform (iOS/Android)
    • Language preference (locale)
  • Purchase Information: Information about in-app purchases and subscriptions managed through RevenueCat, including purchase status and subscription entitlements. This is necessary to restore your purchases and manage subscriptions. Payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store), and we do not have access to your payment details.
  • Advertising Identifier: On iOS, we may access the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) if you have granted permission. On Android, we may access the Advertising ID (AAID). These identifiers are used by our advertising partners (Google AdMob) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can reset or disable these identifiers in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To restore your app state, energy balance, and purchases when you reinstall the app
  • To send push notifications about completed generations and app updates (with your permission)
  • To process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements (through Google AdMob)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and ensure app security

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our Service. Each service has its own privacy policy:

3.1. Supabase (Backend Services)

We use Supabase to store minimal technical data necessary for app functionality:

  • Anonymous device identifier
  • Push notification tokens
  • Usage statistics (energy balance, generation count)
  • Purchase restoration data

Data is stored securely on Supabase servers, which may be located in the United States or other countries. Supabase is GDPR compliant and processes data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

3.2. fal.ai (AI Processing)

To enable artificial intelligence features, we use the third-party service fal.ai:

  • When you create a generation request, your input data (prompts or photos) is transmitted through a secure connection to the fal.ai API
  • This data is used exclusively to fulfill your request and is not stored by fal.ai after processing
  • You can review fal.ai's privacy policy here: fal.ai Privacy Policy

3.3. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • AdMob may collect technical device identifiers (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • AdMob may also collect approximate location data (country/region level) for ad targeting
  • AdMob may collect device information (device type, OS version) for ad delivery
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)
  • Review Google's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.4. RevenueCat (Purchase Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions:

  • RevenueCat stores purchase information and subscription status
  • We use an anonymous RevenueCat User ID to restore your purchases without linking to personal information
  • RevenueCat processes payment information through Apple App Store (for iOS) or Google Play Store (for Android)
  • We do not have access to your payment details; all payment processing is handled by Apple or Google
  • Review RevenueCat's privacy policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

3.5. Apple App Store (iOS Purchases)

For iOS users, in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store:

  • Apple processes all payments and handles payment information
  • We receive only purchase confirmation and subscription status from Apple
  • We do not have access to your Apple ID, payment methods, or billing information
  • Review Apple's privacy policy: Apple Privacy Policy
  • Review Apple's App Store terms: App Store Terms

3.6. Google Play Store (Android Purchases)

For Android users, in-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through the Google Play Store:

  • Google processes all payments and handles payment information
  • We receive only purchase confirmation and subscription status from Google
  • We do not have access to your Google account, payment methods, or billing information
  • Review Google Play's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Review Google Play's terms: Google Play Terms

3.7. Google ML Kit (Face Detection)

We use Google ML Kit Face Detection to detect faces in photos you upload:

  • Face detection is performed locally on your device
  • No face data is transmitted to external servers
  • Face detection is used only to improve generation quality
  • Review Google ML Kit privacy information: Google ML Kit Terms

4. Device Permissions

The following permissions are required for the App to function:

  • Photos and Videos (Gallery Access): To select photos that will be used as a base for generation, and to save the finished results to your device. We do not access photos you do not explicitly select.
  • Camera: To take photos directly within the app for use in AI generation.
  • Microphone: To record audio when creating videos with sound.
  • Notifications (Push Notifications): We use notifications to inform you when videos/photos are ready (since AI generation may take some time), as well as for messages about new presets or features. You can disable them at any time in your device settings (iOS: Settings > Notifications > YOU.AI; Android: Settings > Apps > YOU.AI > Notifications).
  • Advertising ID:
    • iOS (IDFA): Required for Google AdMob to display relevant advertisements. You can limit ad tracking in Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking.
    • Android (AAID): Required for Google AdMob to display relevant advertisements. You can reset your advertising ID in Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Where Your Data is Stored:

  • Technical data (device identifier, usage statistics) is stored on Supabase servers, which may be located in the United States, European Union, or other countries outside your country of residence
  • Generated content (photos/videos) is stored only on your device after delivery
  • Input photos used for generation are not stored on our servers
  • Purchase information is stored by RevenueCat and the respective app store (Apple App Store or Google Play Store)

Data Retention:

  • We retain your technical data (device identifier, usage statistics) for as long as necessary to provide the Service and restore your app state
  • If you uninstall the app, your data may remain on our servers for up to 90 days before automatic deletion
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Contact section)
  • Purchase information is retained by Apple or Google in accordance with their respective policies

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • We use secure authentication methods for API access
  • We regularly review and update our security practices
  • However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security

12. Data Deletion

Since we use anonymous identifiers and do not require account creation, you can effectively delete your data by:

Note: Server-side data may be retained for up to 90 days after uninstallation for technical and legal purposes (e.g., fraud prevention, legal compliance), after which it will be automatically deleted. Purchase information is retained by Apple or Google in accordance with their respective retention policies.
