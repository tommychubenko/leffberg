Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smoke Victory ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent user experience.

1. Information Collection and Use

Smoke Victory is designed to function as a habit tracker. We prioritize user privacy, therefore:

  • No Personal Account: We do not require account creation, email addresses, or names.
  • Local Storage: All data related to your habits and timers is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide advertisement and subscription features, the App uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:

  • AdMob (Google): Used for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (like IDFA or AAID) and usage data to show relevant ads. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat helps us verify your purchase status using anonymous identifiers. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics tools (like Firebase Analytics or Flurry) to track your behavior within the App. Your interactions remain private.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all habit data is stored locally on your device:

  • Manual Deletion: You can reset or delete all your data at any time using the "Reset" button within the App settings.
  • App Uninstallation: Uninstalling the App will automatically delete all data associated with it from your device.
